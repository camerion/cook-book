Carrot Bacon

Prep Time: 15 Min

Cook Time: 10 Min

Total Time: 25 Min

Carrot Bacon is trending all over social media and everyone loves it!

Sourced from:sproutliving.com

Ingredients

  • 2 long carrot
  • 2 Tbsp Maple Syrup
  • 1/2 tsp liquid smoke
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400° F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Slice carrots into long strips, or peel strips with a vegetable peeler.
  3. Mix maple syrup, liquid smoke, and cumin in a bowl large enough to marinate the carrots.
  4. Let carrots marinate for about 3 minutes each side or dregde through mixture till completely covered.
  5. Sprinkle with a little salt.
  6. Bake for 7-10 minutes. (The long you bake the crispier).

Basic Tofu Scramble

Prep Time: 5 Min

Cook Time: 10 Min

Total Time: 15 Min

Simple and also vegan friendly scramble that can be altered anyway you like it.

Sorced from: sweetsimplevegan.com

Ingredients

  • 1 block extra firm tofu, pressed and drained
  • 1/4 cup water, to sauté
  • 1/3 cup finely diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 Tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp black salt(kala namak)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. In a medium skillet heat 2 Tbsp of water, add the onions and garlic, and sauté for 3-4 mins.
  2. Add the nutritional yeast to pan, and toast for about 1 min, continuously stir, to not burn.
  3. With clean hands, finely crumble tofu into the pan and mix until well combined. Evenly sprinkle the turmeric. Cook until egg mixture has dried out to your desired consitency.
  4. Remove scramble from heat and mix in the kala namak(black salt). Season to taste and serve warm. Enjoy!

Vegan Pancakes

Prep Time: 10 Min

Cook Time: 10 Min

Total Time: 20 Min

Easy vegan pancakes, 6 fluffy cakes anyway you want to them.

Sorced from:Cookieandkate.com

Ingredients

  • 1 C flour
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 C plant-based milk
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil or oil of choice
  • 2 Tbsp maple syrup or a sweetner
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. In a large bowl mix together dry ingredients. In a separate bowl blend together wet ingredients.
  2. Combine wet ingredients with dry. If adding mix-ins, this is when you fold them in. Let batter rest for 5 mins(turns it fluffy)
  3. If needed lightly, spray or grease skillet. Heat skillet over medium-low heat
  4. Scoop 1/4 C of batter onto skillet. Flip when bubbles form and pop. Cook other side about 1 min.
  5. Repeat with remaining batter, may need to add more oil to your skillet as you go or even adjust you eat as well.
